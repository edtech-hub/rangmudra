Shipping Policy

Processing Time

Each Rangmudra piece is handcrafted to order. Please allow 3–5 business days for your order to be prepared, quality-checked, and packaged before dispatch. During peak seasons or for custom workshop-related orders, processing may take up to 7 business days.

Delivery Timelines

Orders within Bengaluru are typically delivered within 2–3 business days after dispatch. Orders to other cities within India take 5–7 business days. We currently ship only within India. International shipping is not available at this time.

Shipping Charges

A flat shipping fee of ₹99 applies to all orders below ₹1,500. Orders of ₹1,500 and above qualify for complimentary shipping. For bulky items or special packaging requests, additional charges may apply and will be communicated before dispatch.

Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via SMS and WhatsApp (if opted in) that allows you to follow your shipment in real time. You can also view your order status in the My Orders section of your profile.

Packaging

We take care to package every order thoughtfully. Products are wrapped in recycled tissue paper, sealed with our branded stamp, and placed in a kraft box or mailer bag. We consciously avoid single-use plastics in our packaging — in keeping with Rangmudra's commitment to sustainable craft.

Damaged or Lost Shipments

If your order arrives damaged, please photograph the packaging and the item and write to us at hello@rangmudra.com within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange a replacement or refund at no additional cost. In the rare event of a lost shipment, we will investigate with the courier and issue a replacement or refund within 10 business days.

Contact
