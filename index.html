WHAT WE DO

Immerse in the Spectrum of Tradition.

Discover the traditional art of block printing and Eco printing at Rangmudra. We are a Bangalore-based studio reviving the age-old craft of hand block printing and eco printing.

Each piece is lovingly hand-printed by skilled artisans using carved wooden blocks dipped in natural dyes derived from plants and minerals. The result is wearable art that carries the imprint of human hands and centuries of heritage.
