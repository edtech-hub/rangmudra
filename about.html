OUR STORY

Where Ancient Patterns Meet Living Heritage

Rangmudra was born from a deep reverence for India's centuries-old textile traditions. Founded in Bangalore, we are a studio committed to preserving and celebrating the art of hand block printing and eco printing — crafts that have been passed down through generations of artisans.

Our name says it all: "Rang" — colour, and "Mudra" — the impression of a hand. Every piece we create carries both: the colour of natural dyes and the literal imprint of human hands on fabric.

We work directly with skilled artisans, ensuring fair wages and a sustainable practice that honours both people and planet.

SUSTAINABILITY

Every decision we make at Rangmudra is filtered through a commitment to sustainability. We source fabrics from certified organic cotton and natural silk producers. Our dyes are derived entirely from plants, minerals, and other natural sources — never synthetic chemicals.

Our workshop waste water is treated and reused. Our packaging is recycled and compostable. We are not just making beautiful things; we are trying to make them responsibly.

In a world of fast fashion, Rangmudra stands for slow, intentional creation — pieces made to last a lifetime and beyond.

OUR TEAM

Carve a design is more than being pretty :)

Our team is a family of passionate artisans, designers, and heritage enthusiasts. Each member brings a unique skill set — from the precise hand of a master block carver to the keen eye of a pattern designer.

We are committed to using only organic fabrics and natural dyes in every piece we create. Our partnership with local artisan communities ensures that ancient skills are preserved while providing sustainable livelihoods.
